Tech Leads

Transforming Ideas Into Reality Through Creativity & Innovation

About

Tech Leads is a social enterprise which educates to effect change through STEM. We teach latest technologies in Programming, Electronics, Robotics and Internet of Things in tertiary schools in Ghana and encourage them to use the knowledge gained to build technological solutions to solve Africa's problems.

Mission

Our mission and core values is to raise the next generation of doers and makers with practical oriented education in Ghana to develop unique solutions in every sector using technology.

Vision

Our vision for the next five years is to grow our organization into ten universities in Ghana to impact about ten thousand students in all, thus, 1000 students from each university. In our five year goals to raise technology entrepreneurs in Africa, we plan to teach the students we reach with practical STEM to effect change and nurture them to develop unique solutions to solve most of Africa’s problems and help turn implementable projects into businesses by producing them on a large scale for the market to generate income to grow the organization as well as the students’ individual technology businesses.

How It Started

Tech Leads started in September, 2016 in Koforidua Technical University where our Founder Joshua Kumi who was not happy with the education system of learning theories without any practicals to apply and innovate, decided to bridge that gap with practical learning using latest technologies. He gathered some friends and started teaching them web development and gradually introduced new programs and maintained their goal of practical education to build technology innovations to solve problems.

Programs

What We Do

We organize a lot of STEM programs throughout the year for free and for a fee. Go through our lists of programs and register to attend.

Internet of Things

We run Internet of Things classes with latest technologies and trained experts to teach the interconnection via the Internet of computing devices embedded in everyday objects, enabling them to send and receive data. Register for this class here

Programming

In this class, we have different sections where we teach Web Development, Software Development, and Mobile Application Development with latest technologies in these fields using various programming languages. Sign up for this class here

Robotics

We teach students how to design, construct, operate and use a robot from scratch as well as their computer systems for the control, sensory feedback, and information processing to develop harmless robots that can replicate human actions for specific purposes using latest technologies. Sign up for this class here

Electronics

We teach our students how to build electrical circuits which involve the active electrical components such as LEDs, breadboards, integrated circuits, optoelectronics, and sensors which are associated with passive electrical components, and interconnection technologies to innovate. Sign up here

Graphics Design

For beginners, intermediate and advanced graphic designers who are willing to learn more about how to design graphics using latest software like PhotoShop, Adobe Lightroom, Corel Draw, etc. for creative and professional designs, join this class. Sign up here

Summer Program

In partnership with Global Code, we bring the world's best software engineers from the UK every summer to teach Programming, Electronics and Internet of Things in our annual summer programs for free. Students get the opportunity to receive mentorship from experts from the software engineering industry globally for free. Sign up here
